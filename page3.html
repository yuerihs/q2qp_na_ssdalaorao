Who We Are?

The protection and advancement of the humane treatment of all animals is the mission of PAWS, a registered non-profit organization (NGO). Regarding issues of animal welfare, PAWS is unwavering in its support of stronger legislation that would guarantee the treatment of all animals in a humane manner.
PAWS main work is to educate and disseminate information concerning animal welfare, animal care, animal control, and other animal-related concerns..

Vision

PAWS is a credible nationwide non-profit organization, composed of a network of committed, compassionate and trustworthy individuals and institutions that initiate and lead in the promotion of animal welfare, and the protection of all animals.
PAWS envisions a nation that respects animals, practices responsible pet ownership, and protects wildlife

Mission

The Philippine Animal Welfare Society exists to prevent cruelty, alleviate pain, fear and suffering in animals and to promote a society based on humane principles.
